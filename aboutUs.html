üìñ Our Story

ImiGames was founded in 2024 by a team of passionate educators, parents, and game developers who saw the need for truly safe and educational online gaming experiences for children. Frustrated by the lack of quality options in the market, we decided to create something different.

üöÄ The Beginning

It all started when our founder's 6-year-old daughter asked to play "fun learning games" online. After hours of searching through countless websites filled with inappropriate ads, violent content, and privacy concerns, we realized there had to be a better way.

üéÆ Building the Platform

We spent over a year researching child development, consulting with educators, and working with parents to understand exactly what kids need from online gaming. The result is ImiGames - a platform built from the ground up with children's safety, education, and enjoyment in mind.
